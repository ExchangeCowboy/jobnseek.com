Bringing the human back.

The job market didn't just get competitive. It got hollow. Candidates send hundreds of applications into a void. Résumés get parsed by machines, filtered by keyword matching, ranked by a score that has nothing to do with whether they can actually do the work. Recruiters drown in noise they never asked for. Hiring managers meet a shortlist assembled by an algorithm and wonder why the right people never seem to make it through.

Somewhere in all of it, the person got lost.

jobnseek isn't trying to rebuild recruiting from scratch. We're fixing the part that matters most: the moment when a person who wants to work meets a person with work to offer. Direct. Human. Real.

On AI

We're not pretending AI doesn't exist. It does, and it will continue to reshape how companies hire. One day, jobnseek will put a recruiter-side AI on this platform, trained by veterans of HR who have spent careers learning the difference between a candidate who looks right on paper and one who actually is.

Not a résumé filter. Not a ranking engine. Something that can hold a thoughtful conversation about a real person's full profile and help a recruiter decide if it's worth a conversation.

But the principle doesn't change: AI in service of human judgment. A tool. Not a gatekeeper.

How it works.

Four steps. No résumé PDF required.

01

Bring your résumé

Upload it. We'll parse it. Edit, expand, or replace any part. It's a starting point, not a ceiling.

02

Add what doesn't fit

Portfolio, side projects, writing, code, things you've built. The work that never makes it onto one page.

03

Take the assessments

Validated characteristics testing. Show how you think, communicate, and work. Not just where you've been.

04

Check in weekly

One click. Skip a week, you go dormant. Recruiters only see active people. No fossils in the results.
